# 📊 Customer Churn & Revenue at Risk Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end data analysis and visualization solution focused on **Customer Churn** and **Revenue at Risk**. Using Power BI and Excel, the analysis evaluates churn rates across customer segments, identifies high-value accounts at risk of churning, and provides data-driven recommendations to improve retention.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives
* **Quantify Churn:** Measure overall churn rates and lost revenue over specific time periods.
* **Identify At-Risk Revenue:** Pinpoint specific customer cohorts and subscription tiers contributing most to revenue loss.
* **Customer Segmentation:** Analyze demographic, behavioral, and contractual drivers behind churn.
* **Executive Dashboard:** Build an intuitive, interactive Power BI dashboard for stakeholders to monitor retention metrics.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
* **Excel:** Initial data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and validation.
* **Power BI:** Data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive dashboard creation.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Calculated columns and dynamic measures for churn rate, lost MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), and customer risk scores.

---

## 💡 Key Metrics & Insights
* **Overall Churn Rate (%):** Percentage of customers lost over time.
* **Total Revenue at Risk:** Monetary value tied to churning or low-engagement accounts.
* **Segment Risk:** Comparison across account types, contract lengths, and tenure groups.
